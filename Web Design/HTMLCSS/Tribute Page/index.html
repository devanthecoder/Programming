Toby Fox

The man who created my childhood obsession

Toby Fox - The visionary creator whose games heavily influenced my childhood and teen years.

Here's a timeline of Fox's life so far

  • 1991: Born in Manchester, New Hampshire.
  • 2008: Developed the 'EarthBound Halloween Hack', a modification of the game EarthBound, gaining initial recognition in the gaming community.
  • 2009–2016: Composed music for the webcomic "Homestuck," contributing significantly to its soundtrack.
  • 2014: Graduated from Northeastern University with a degree in Environmental Science.
  • 2015: Released Undertale, an indie role-playing game that achieved critical acclaim for its innovative gameplay and storytelling.
  • 2016: Undertale received nominations for a British Academy Game Award, three Game Awards, and D.I.C.E. Awards.
  • 2017: Composed music for the game Hiveswap, a spinoff of Homestuck.
  • 2018: Released Chapter 1 of Deltarune, a free spiritual successor to Undertale.
  • 2019:
    • Composed music for Game Freak's RPG Little Town Hero, with arrangements by Pokémon composer Hitomi Sato.
    • Composed a battle track for Pokémon Sword and Shield.
  • 2021: Released Chapter 2 of Deltarune for free
  • 2022: Composed the field music for Pokémon Scarlet and Violet.
  • 2024:
    • Partnered with Japanese game developer ZUN for the game Touhou Danmaku Kagura Phantasia Lost, creating a track titled "U.N. Owen Was Hero?".
    • Announced that Chapters 3 and 4 of Deltarune are nearing completion, with professional testing and localization underway, aiming for an early 2025 release.

Toby Fox's journey from a passionate game developer and composer to an influential figure in the gaming industry highlights his creativity and dedication.
